## Fix 4: Meditated Mech (NEW FEATURE)

### Spawn Logic
- **When**: After box breathing meditation completes (all 6 cycles)
- **Where**: Good Memories quadrant (top-left)

### Visual Design
- **Shape**: Large octagon (distinct from other unit types)
- **Color**: Golden (#F5D76E) with bright glow/aura
- **Size**: 1.5x larger than artillery
- **Icon**: 🧘 emoji marker
- **Aura**: Pulsing glow radius (breathing animation)

### Behavior
- **Does NOT fire projectiles** (support/defensive unit only)
- **Global effect**: Reduces ALL units' fire rate by 40%
- **Implementation**: Multiply all fire delays by 1.67x (1 / 0.6)
- **Duration**: **Adjustable by user** (see below)
- **Countdown**: Show remaining time "🧘 Mech: 2:34"

### Duration System (NEW - User Adjustable)

**Option A: Manual Slider (Recommended)**
- UI control when Mech spawns or in settings
- Options:
  - Short: 1 minute
  - Medium: 3 minutes (default)
  - Long: 5 minutes
  - Extended: 10 minutes
- User picks duration before/after meditation

**Option B: Tied to Meditation Length**
- 2 cycles → 1 min Mech
- 4 cycles → 2 min Mech
- 6 cycles → 3 min Mech
- 10+ cycles → 5 min Mech
- Rewards longer practice

**Implement**: Option A (manual slider) for v7.1 - simpler, more control

### Decay & Expiration
- Slowly shrinks/fades over lifetime (visual countdown)
- Final 30 seconds: starts flickering
- On expiration: particle effect, message "Meditation effect faded"
- Fire rates return to normal over 5 seconds (smooth ramp)

### Stacking Rules
- Only 1 Mech can exist at a time
- Meditating again while active: reset timer to selected duration
- Show message: "Meditation renewed ✨"

### UI Display
- **Thought list**: Special styling with golden background
  - "🧘 Meditated Mech (2:15 remaining)"
  - Can't be manually removed (expires naturally)
- **Screen indicator**: "🧘 -40% battlefield intensity"
- **Duration selector**: Dropdown or slider before meditation ends

### First-Time Message
- "Your meditation created lasting peace. The Meditated Mech will calm your battlefield."
